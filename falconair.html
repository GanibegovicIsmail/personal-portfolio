Overview

Falcon Air is a imaginary airline company. Visual designs regarding this project had to include certain types of orange that represent joy. Also there are several different types of mockups that really show how the potential airline company might look like.

Context

Falcon Air was a part of a university project that I did on my own. Students had to continue with the theme of our other projects in that given subject so I choose to go with the airline company which was really the best choice in that given situation.
The other reason I choose to go with the airline company is because the dream of most graphic designers is to have their design presented on really large object such as airplane.

Logo

I was considering a unique logo design for an airline company and had the idea to feature a falcon as the primary element. To bring this concept to life, I sourced an image of a soaring falcon and traced a simple outline in my sketchbook. Subsequently, I imported this sketch into Adobe Illustrator to create a polished 3D representation of the falcon as the final logo design for the company.

Subsequently, I determined that a two-dimensional logo would be the most suitable for the company. Thus, I returned to the original logo design and transformed the three-dimensional logo into a two-dimensional format, which greatly enhanced its usability.

Other than the falcon as a hidden meaning in the logo. The graphical representation of the logo signifies that the prominent lines in the picture depict the easily accessible routes, while the delicate and subdued lines signify destinations that are less accessible by other air carriers.

Brochure / Flayer

The brochure provides an overview of the airline company, including its history and mission. The "About Us" section highlights the company's achievements and commitment to quality and customer service. The "Our Team" section features the dedicated employees who work to ensure a safe and comfortable travel experience for passengers. The brochure also includes information on safety and quality guarantees, as well as details on how to contact the company for any inquiries or concerns. In addition, the brochure promotes the convenience of online booking and highlights the friendly and helpful nature of the airline's staff.

Visiting Card


The visiting card of the airline company features a simple and elegant design, with a light gray front page that provides general information about the company. The front page is accented with subtle touches of orange, creating a sophisticated look. On the back page, the design takes on a bolder look, with a vibrant orange color that really pops. This design showcases the company's commitment to style and innovation, making it the perfect representation of the brand.
