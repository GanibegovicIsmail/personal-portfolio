Overview

The trend of reading books has been on a decline in recent times, due to the increase in alternative forms of entertainment and the discomfort in reading books on screens, which lacks the personal experience of reading a physical book. Cozy Library is a platform that provides a comprehensive solution to this issue. It allows users to select books based on their preferences and offers a detailed description of each book along with its categorization.

This web-based application enables users to make informed decisions when it comes to purchasing books. By providing them with a comprehensive overview of each book, including its description and cost, users can easily select the book of their choice and make the purchase online.

Context

Cozy Library is my latest project, where I have employed AI technology to assist me in the development of the website. The aim was to utilize AI as a source of inspiration and to enhance the overall user experience of the platform.
