Overview

With so many different rules depending on what you find or somebody tells you, it can get really confusing to follow the best practices when it comes to becomming fit. To adress this problem, Aptus was designed to take in consideration the users current phisical status and personalize the information depening on it.

Main functionalities: an intuitive fitness app that depening on the user's entry information gives correct feedback and advice for improving that individual's fitness capabilities.
