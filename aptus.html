Overview

With so many different rules depending on what you find or somebody tells you, it can get really confusing to follow the best practices when it comes to becomming fit. To adress this problem, Aptus was designed to take in consideration the users current phisical status and personalize the information depening on it.

It has two key functionalities: Giving users feedback and advice depening on the calculated user's BMI. Has the ability of gender selection and based on that features different styles of workout.

Context

Aptus is a simple fitness app that I developet, from conception to execution, for a college project. It was mainly designed to be simple and likeable by beginners who are this app's targeted audience.
